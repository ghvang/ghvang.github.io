FAQ

  • What is your cancellation policy?

    We ask that there be at least a 36 hour notice before your appointment. If you cancel your appointment within 36 hours of your time slot, we will keep your deposit.

  • How do I book an appointment?

    You can call the parlor or book online to scheduled appointments with your artist of choice. If you do not have a preferred artist, we will assign you to whoever is available.

  • Are payment plans available?

    To be approved of a payment plan, you need to first put down a 30% non-refundable deposit. You must pay the full price of the tattoo or scheduled session by the time of your appointment. If you have any concerns or questions, please call our parlor.

  • How much are tattoos? What are your rates?

    Rates are determined by the individual artists. If you want to discuss prices or ask specific questions, please contact the artist you would like to book. If you do not have a preferred artist, you can call our parlor for further questions and discussion.
